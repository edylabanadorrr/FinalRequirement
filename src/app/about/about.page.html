BATELEC II

The Batangas II Electric Cooperative, Inc.(BATELEC II), is one of the two (2) Electric Cooperatives that distribute electricity to Batangas province with its principal office at Antipolo del Norte, Lipa City. It was organized and duly registered with the National Electrification Administration(NEA) on Aug 12, 1977.
